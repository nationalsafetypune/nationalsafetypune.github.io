What We Offer

Our Services

End-to-end fire safety engineering — design, supply, installation and compliance.

01

Fire Detection & Alarm Systems

A reliable fire detection and alarm system is the first line of defence. NFPSE designs, supplies and commissions intelligent detection networks that respond in seconds — minimising damage and saving lives.

  • Addressable and conventional fire alarm control panels
  • Smoke, heat and multi-sensor detectors
  • Manual call points, sounders and visual alarms
  • Integration with building management systems (BMS)
  • Central monitoring and remote alert connectivity
  • Annual maintenance contracts (AMC)
02

Fire Suppression Systems

When a fire ignites, suppression systems act immediately to contain and extinguish it — protecting high-value assets and occupants alike. We design systems matched precisely to risk class and occupancy type.

  • Wet and dry pipe sprinkler systems (IS 15105)
  • Clean-agent systems: FM-200, NOVEC 1230, CO2
  • Foam suppression for fuel storage and aircraft hangars
  • Water mist systems for heritage buildings and data centres
  • Pre-action systems for sensitive environments
  • Hydraulic calculations and design documentation
03

Hydrant & Water Supply Systems

Hydrant infrastructure forms the backbone of any large-scale fire response. NFPSE engineers compliant external and internal hydrant networks to IS and NBC standards, ensuring fire services can access water instantly.

  • External yard hydrant networks for industrial complexes
  • Internal wet-riser and down-comer systems
  • Pump sets: electric, diesel and jockey pumps
  • Underground and overhead storage tank sizing
  • Fire brigade inlet connections and landing valves
  • Pressure calculations and flow testing
04

Safety Audits, AMC & NOC Services

Maintaining compliance is not a one-time event. NFPSE provides structured audit programmes, annual maintenance contracts and complete NOC application support — so your fire systems remain fully operational and legally compliant year-round.

  • Fire safety statutory audits and gap analysis reports
  • NOC application preparation and authority liaison
  • Annual maintenance contracts for all installed systems
  • Third-party inspection coordination
  • Corrective action reports and remediation planning
  • Documentation management for regulatory submissions
05

Fire Safety Training & Mock Drills

Even the best systems require informed occupants to operate optimally during an emergency. NFPSE delivers practical, scenario-based training programmes designed for real-world readiness.

  • Certified fire warden and marshal training
  • Occupant evacuation drills and debrief sessions
  • Portable extinguisher handling and usage training
  • Emergency response plan (ERP) development
  • Tabletop scenario exercises for management teams
  • Post-drill reports with improvement recommendations
06

Fire Engineering & Consulting

Complex projects demand performance-based fire engineering solutions that go beyond prescriptive codes. NFPSE provides expert consulting from concept stage through to handover — bridging the gap between design intent and regulatory approval.

  • Performance-based fire engineering (PBFE) studies
  • Fire hazard analysis and risk assessment
  • Peer review of third-party fire system designs
  • Project management and contractor coordination
  • Liaison with fire departments and approval authorities
  • Post-occupancy fire safety reviews

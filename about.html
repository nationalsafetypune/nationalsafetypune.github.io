About NFPSE

15+ years of protecting lives, assets and futures through expert fire safety engineering.

Our Story

Built on Purpose,
Proven in the Field

National Fire Protection and Safety Engineers (NFPSE) was founded with one mission: to make every building, factory and public space safer through professional fire engineering. Over a decade of projects across Maharashtra and beyond has reinforced that mission with hard-earned expertise.

We are a government-recognised, Licensed Agency staffed by qualified fire safety engineers, system designers and project managers who understand the full life-cycle of fire protection — from regulatory design to post-installation maintenance.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, code-compliant fire protection systems and safety services that genuinely protect people, property and business continuity — without compromise on quality or safety margins.

Our Vision

To be India’s most trusted fire safety engineering partner — recognised for technical excellence, regulatory integrity and the depth of partnerships we build with every client.

Our Values

Safety above all. Transparency in every recommendation. Accountability at each project stage. Continuous learning to stay ahead of evolving codes and technologies.

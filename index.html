About NFPSE

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Safety

National Fire Protection and Safety Engineers (NFPSE) is a government-approved, ISO-certified fire engineering firm with over 15 years of field-proven experience. We serve commercial, industrial and residential clients across India with end-to-end fire safety solutions.

From concept design to site execution and regulatory compliance — every project is handled by qualified engineers committed to protecting lives and assets.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Six pillars of professional fire safety engineering.

Fire Detection & Alarm

Advanced smoke, heat and multi-sensor detection networks with centralised monitoring panels.

Suppression Systems

Sprinkler networks, clean-agent gaseous systems and foam suppression for high-hazard zones.

Hydrant Systems

External and internal hydrant infrastructure designed to IS/NBC standards for quick-access water supply.

Safety Audits & NOC

Comprehensive statutory audits, AMC services and NOC application support with authority liaison.

Training & Drills

Certified occupant training programmes, mock drills and evacuation planning for all building types.

Fire Engineering & Consulting

Performance-based fire engineering, design review, hazard analysis and project management.
